Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2, 2026

This Privacy Policy governs your use of the mobile game application Gravity Runner , created by misvansoftwares.

The Application is a physics-based, gravity-defying runner game designed for mobile devices. We are completely committed to protecting and respecting your data privacy privacy while you enjoy the game.

1. Information We Collect

A. Automatically Collected Information

The Application may automatically collect certain non-personal technical data during gameplay to ensure stability and performance, including:

  • Your mobile device type and hardware specifications.
  • Your operating system version (e.g., Android version).
  • The device's unique anonymous advertising identifier (e.g., Google AAID).
  • Anonymized game performance metrics, such as high scores, levels achieved, session lengths, and technical crash logs.

B. Personal Data

We do not collect, store, or solicit any personal information that can directly identify you—such as your name, email address, physical location, or phone number. If you choose to contact us directly for customer support, your email address will only be used to respond to your inquiry and will be permanently deleted once the support ticket is resolved.

2. Third-Party Services and Data Usage

To keep the application highly stable and optimize performance, we utilize industry-standard third-party services. These tools may access your technical data to help us monitor game health.

These third-party platforms include:

  • Google Play Services / Firebase: For real-time crash reporting, performance tracking, analytics, and managing global high-score leaderboards.
  • Ad Networks (e.g., Google AdMob): If the game displays advertisements, these third-party networks may use your device's anonymized advertising ID to serve relevant, contextual, or personalized ads depending entirely on your individual device permissions.

Device Permissions Note: The Application requests network access permissions exclusively to sync online features like leaderboards, fetch cloud updates, or load ad inventory securely.

3. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

We do not knowingly collect or maintain personal information from anyone under the age of 13. The Application is built from the ground up to be completely safe for players of all ages. If we learn that a child under 13 has inadvertently shared personal information, we will immediately wipe that data from our records.

4. Your Data Control and Opt-Outs

You retain full control over the technical data shared from your mobile device:

  • Stop All Data Collection: You can completely halt all automated technical data collection instantly by uninstalling the Application from your device.
  • Manage Advertising Identifiers: You can reset your device's advertising tracking identifiers or opt completely out of interest-based ads via your core Android settings menu (Settings > Google > Ads > Delete/Reset Advertising ID).

5. Data Security

We prioritize the safety of your information. Because we explicitly avoid collecting sensitive personal data, we use standard electronic and procedural security safeguards to safely protect the technical, anonymized performance metrics processed by the Application.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may periodically update this Privacy Policy to reflect updates to the game or changes in global privacy regulations. Any updates will be pushed directly to this live link, and the "Last Updated" date at the top of the page will change accordingly.
